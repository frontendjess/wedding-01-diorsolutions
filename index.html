Our Story

The moment I met Andrea at a conference, I knew she was someone special. She became my first love. We maintained our love affair through letters, phone calls, and weekend visits. But geography took its toll, and she ended up moving even further away to pursue her doctorates. I spent the next years thinking of her often. One day, I just knew I could not wait to spend another day without the love of my life. So I surprised her and we reunited, we finally embraced one another after a long six years. After all those years apart, we have decided to marry and celebrate our love.
